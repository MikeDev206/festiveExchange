# festiveExchange
Simple app to register people for the exchange, organize and randomly assign who you have to give a gift to.

## How It Works
This app helps you organize a Secret Santa or festive gift exchange:
1. **Register participants** - Add people who want to participate in the gift exchange
2. **Set up the exchange** - Configure any rules or preferences for the exchange
3. **Random assignment** - The app randomly assigns each participant to give a gift to another person
4. **Everyone gets matched** - Each person is assigned someone to give a gift to, ensuring everyone receives a gift
